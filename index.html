Why LogoGrid

The contrast checks your artboard hides

A logo that looks perfect on white can vanish on gray, glow weirdly on a gradient, or get lost in a photo. See every case in one glance.

Light & dark, side by side

White, off-white, charcoal, true black. Confirm your mark reads in both modes without rebuilding a mockup.

The 50% gray trap

Mid-gray is where most logos die: too dark for the light version, too light for the dark one. We put it front and center.

Busy photo backgrounds

Concrete and foliage textures reveal whether your logo needs a plate, a shadow, or a knockout to stay legible over imagery.

Tune size & padding live

Drag the sliders and every tile updates instantly. Find the clear-space that keeps the mark breathing on each surface.
